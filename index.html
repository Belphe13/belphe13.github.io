Lilan Yang (b. Chongqing, China) is an artist and experimental filmmaker, whose work delves into the flux of migration, the decay of memories and the intricacies of perception. Their work, rooted in 16mm filmmaking, is further enriched by machine learning and data visualization, exploring the materiality and temporality of film. Yang currently lives and works in Boston.
