I Woke Up in the Morning observes a gentle journey from a cityscape into a desertscape. The voyage from the populated city of Boston in the winter to the unoccupied Chihuahuan desert in the summer narrates the constant state of dissatisfaction regarding one's position in time and space. The traveling voice tells a story of longing, realizing that the things one wishes to preserve have already slipped away.

- Texts by Veronica Bello and Corina Dorrego
