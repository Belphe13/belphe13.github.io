...Yang transcends mere homage, filtering the mapping exercise of their film-inspired road trip into a borderline pointillist tapestry of barely recognisable iconography. The effect of the distortion is uncanny: like memories, these blown-up, high-contrast scenes are somehow clearer when viewed from afar - as if proximity is merely an illusion.
